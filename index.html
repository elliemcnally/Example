Hazy Days: A Breathing Simulator

Introduction

Hazy Days, created by Mike Ren, is an unforgettable game that immerses players in the harsh realities of living in a smog-filled world. Through Xiao Feng, a young girl navigating her daily life in a polluted city, players experience the silent yet pervasive challenges of breathing in such an environment. It is a hauntingly beautiful reflection of the realities faced by millions worldwide.

Why "Hazy Days" Matters

Air pollution steals breath, health, and hope from countless lives, and Hazy Days captures this struggle with heart-wrenching clarity. The game is not just a simulation—it is a cry for awareness, empathy, and change. It teaches us that what we take for granted, like clean air, is a dream for many. Through Xiao Feng’s journey, players feel the weight of each breath, reminding us of our shared humanity and responsibility.

Visual Aesthetics

The game’s visuals blend simplicity and emotional depth. Its muted palette and soft edges evoke a poignant sense of despair and beauty, mirroring the fragile balance between life and the toxic environment. Xiao Feng’s delicate figure against the smog-heavy backdrop tells a silent but powerful story of resilience.

Mini Games in Hazy Days

Breathing Control: This mini-game challenges players to guide Xiao Feng through the treacherous streets filled with smog. Every breath matters, and every inhale of polluted air weighs heavily on her health. It is a stark reminder of how pollution affects even the most basic human function.

Home Activities: Even at home, Xiao Feng isn’t safe from the invisible threat of pollution. This mini-game portrays the constant vigilance required to maintain health indoors, as players manage her activities like eating and studying while monitoring air quality levels.

School Commute: Xiao Feng’s journey to school is fraught with obstacles—from crowded buses to polluted streets. This mini-game emphasizes how the environment seeps into every aspect of daily life, turning ordinary routines into perilous challenges.

Health Monitoring: In this mini-game, players must make critical decisions to protect Xiao Feng’s health. Balancing rest, activity, and exposure to pollution, it paints a vivid picture of the cumulative impact of living in such conditions, leaving players deeply moved by her resilience.
